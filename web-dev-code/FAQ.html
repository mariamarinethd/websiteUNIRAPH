Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions from our users.

How do I book a ride?

Go to "Find Rides", enter your locations and date, and select a verified driver to book your ride.

How can I offer a ride?

Go to "Offer Rides", provide your route, vehicle details, seats, and price, then post your ride.

Is it safe to share rides?

All drivers and passengers are verified. Follow safety guidelines to ensure a secure journey.

How do I pay?

Payments are agreed directly with the driver. Cashless methods may be added soon for convenience.
