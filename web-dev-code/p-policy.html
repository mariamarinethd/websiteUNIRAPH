Privacy Policy

At UniraPh, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform.

Information We Collect

  • Personal details like name, email, and contact information.
  • Ride details including pick-up, destination, and preferences.
  • Usage data to improve our services and user experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and manage ride-sharing services.
  • To communicate with users about their rides and updates.
  • To improve our platform and customize your experience.

Data Protection

We use industry-standard security measures to safeguard your data and prevent unauthorized access. Your information is never sold to third parties.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance functionality and user experience. You can manage cookie settings in your browser.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with the revised date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at @uniraphrides.com.
