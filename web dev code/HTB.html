Tips for New Commuters

Welcome to UniraPh! Follow these tips to ensure a safe, comfortable, and smooth ride.

Safety Tips

  • Always verify driver and rider profiles before confirming a ride.
  • Share your trip details with a trusted friend or family member.
  • Use public pickup points when meeting a driver for the first time.

Ride Etiquette

  • Arrive on time and respect the driver’s schedule.
  • Keep the vehicle clean; avoid eating messy foods during the ride.
  • Communicate politely and clearly when coordinating ride details.

Best Practices

  • Confirm your ride early to secure your spot.
  • Prepare exact payment if required.
  • Provide feedback after your ride to improve the UniraPh community.
