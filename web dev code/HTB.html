How to Booking

Step-by-step guide to booking your ride with UniraPh quickly and safely.

Step 1: Find a Ride

Search for rides by entering your starting point, destination, and travel date.

Step 2: Connect with Driver

Choose a verified driver and communicate through the app to confirm details.

Step 3: Secure Payment

Pay securely through the platform or directly to the driver as per your preference.

Step 4: Enjoy Your Ride

Meet your driver, board safely, and enjoy a comfortable journey.
